FEELNGS
  1. Pick your adventure! Tap a quiz game to launch the fun.
  2. Listen closely: the question will be read out loud!
  3. Repeat, please! Tap the button to replay the question.
  4. Need quiet time? You can toggle the audio ON or OFF using the speaker button on the Home Page.
  5. Choose wisely! Four answers will pop up for your little one to select.
  6. Hooray! A correct answer gets a burst of positive feedback, and then it's straight to the next question.
  7. Oops! An incorrect answer gets a small nudge to show it wasn't quite right.
  8. If your child misses three in a row, the correct answer will gently shimmer to encourage self-correction.
  9. Ready for a new challenge? Hit the Home button (top left) to pick a different quiz.
  10. With a little guidance and practice, your little explorer should be navigating the game all by themselves!
  11. Enjoy the journey as you watch your child learn and grow!
